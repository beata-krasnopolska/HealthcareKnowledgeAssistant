# Patient Visit Preparation

## General Guidance

Patients are encouraged to arrive at least 15 minutes before their scheduled appointment time. This helps complete registration, verify insurance details, and avoid delays.

Please bring:
- a valid photo ID,
- your insurance card,
- a list of current medications,
- any referral documents if required,
- previous relevant test results if available.

## Blood Test Preparation

For standard fasting blood tests, patients should not eat for 8 to 12 hours before the test unless instructed otherwise by their clinician. Drinking water is usually allowed and recommended.

Patients should avoid intense exercise and alcohol consumption the day before a fasting blood test unless a healthcare professional has provided different instructions.

## Imaging Preparation

Preparation requirements for imaging depend on the type of exam. Some scans may require fasting, hydration, or removal of metal objects. Patients should carefully follow the instructions provided at the time of scheduling.

## Late Arrival Policy

If a patient arrives more than 15 minutes late, the appointment may need to be rescheduled depending on provider availability.
