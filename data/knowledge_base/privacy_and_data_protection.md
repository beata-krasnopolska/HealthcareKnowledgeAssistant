# Privacy and Data Protection

## Confidentiality

The clinic is committed to protecting patient privacy and handling personal health information in a confidential and secure manner.

Access to patient information is limited to authorized staff members who need the information to perform their job responsibilities.

## Medical Records Access

Patients may request access to their medical records by submitting a written request through the medical records department. Identity verification may be required before records are released.

## Data Sharing

Patient information may be shared with other healthcare providers, laboratories, or insurers only when necessary for treatment, payment, or healthcare operations, or when otherwise permitted by law.

## Security Measures

The clinic applies administrative, technical, and physical safeguards to reduce the risk of unauthorized access, disclosure, or misuse of patient information.

## Contact for Privacy Questions

Questions related to privacy practices and data protection should be directed to the clinic privacy office.
