# Clinic FAQ

## What should I bring to my first visit?

Please bring a photo ID, insurance card, medication list, and any referral or previous test documentation that may be relevant to your visit.

## Can I get test results by phone?

Some routine results may be communicated through the patient portal or by phone, depending on clinic policy and the nature of the result.

## What if I am late?

If you are more than 15 minutes late, your appointment may need to be rescheduled.

## Does the clinic provide emergency care?

No. The clinic does not provide emergency care. Patients experiencing a medical emergency should call local emergency services or go to the nearest emergency department.

## Can family members access my records?

Access to patient records is subject to privacy rules, identity verification, authorization, and applicable law.
