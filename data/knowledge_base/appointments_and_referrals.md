# Appointments and Referrals

## Scheduling Appointments

Patients can schedule appointments by phone, through the patient portal, or in person at the reception desk.

## Rescheduling and Cancellation

If you need to cancel or reschedule an appointment, please contact the clinic at least 24 hours in advance whenever possible.

Repeated missed appointments may affect future scheduling priority.

## Referrals

Some specialist visits may require a referral from a primary care provider, depending on the type of service and insurance requirements.

Patients are encouraged to confirm referral requirements before booking specialist services.

## Same-Day Visits

A limited number of same-day appointments may be available for urgent but non-life-threatening concerns. Availability depends on clinic capacity.
